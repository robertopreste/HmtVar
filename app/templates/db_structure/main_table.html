Locus Type

Locus type to which the variant belongs: Coding sequences (CDS), Regulatory sequences, rRNA, tRNA.

Mutation

Specific mutation, in the formats
  • [ref_nt][position][alt_nt] (e.g. T3308A)
  • [ref_nt][position] (e.g. T3308)
  • [position][alt_nt] (e.g. 3308A)

Position

Variants occurring in one or more positions in the mitochondrial genome, in the formats
  • [position] (single position)
  • [pos_1,pos_2,pos_3] (list of positions)
  • [start_pos-end_pos] (range of positions)

Locus

Variants occurring in one specific mitochondrial locus.

Codon Position

In case of a Coding Sequence variant, the codon position it belongs to.

Aminoacid Change

In case of a Coding Sequence variant, the aminoacidic change it determines.

Disease Score

The variant disease score calculated by HmtVar, in the formats
  • [dis_score] (specific value)
  • [min_score-max_score] (range of disease scores)

tRNA Model

In case of a tRNA variant, the tRNA model it belongs to.

Pathogenicity

The variant pathogenicity predicted by HmtVar: Likely Pathogenic, Likely Polymorphic, Pathogenic, Polymorphic.
